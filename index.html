About Sri Devi Textiles

Traditional & Modern Collection since 1998

Our Story

Founded in the heart of Chennai's textile district, Sri Devi Textiles has been a beacon of quality and craftsmanship for over two decades.

Every piece tells a story — of skilled artisans, time-honored techniques, and the vibrant culture of Indian textiles.

Our mission: bring the elegance of Indian textiles to every wardrobe without compromising authenticity.
